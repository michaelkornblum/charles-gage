Welcome

Thank you for visiting my website. I have been a piano tuner and rebuilder since 1976. With over forty years of experience, I will be able to take care of all of your pianos' needs. I have apprenticed in piano rebuilding shops in New York City, attended the Steinway factory school and am a proud member of the Piano Technician's Guild.

I am able to serve customers throughout the Greater Memphis Metropolitan area including, Germantown, Cellreville, Marion, Blytheville and Osceola.
